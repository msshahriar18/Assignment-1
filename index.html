Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about the conference? We've got you covered.

When and where is DevConf 2026 taking place?

DevConf 2026 will be held from August 15-17, 2026. It features three action-packed days of keynotes, interactive tracks, and networking mixers with industry leaders.

Will the conference sessions be recorded?

Yes, all main-stage talks and panels will be professionally recorded. Access to these digital recordings is included for 30 days with the Pro and Team registration passes.

Are there group discounts available for teams?

Absolutely! Our "Team" pass covers up to 10 attendees and offers a significant discount per person, along with premium perks like dedicated lounge access and recruitment booths.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes, tickets can be transferred to a colleague or friend up to 7 days before the event begins. Simply drop us an email via our support portal with your booking reference details.
